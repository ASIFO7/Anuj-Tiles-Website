Distinguished Achievements

Pioneers in Ceramic Industry

Aravind Ceramics holds ISO 9001-2000 certification from "TUV NORD", Germany, signifying its commitment to excellence in Quality Management Systems.

Our dedication to quality and excellence has been recognized through numerous prestigious awards:

  • KSPC Productivity Award (1983, 1989)
  • Indian Institute of Materials Management's 2nd prize in store management competition
  • National Institute of Quality and Reliability (1991)
  • KSIDC Award for excellence in Kerala Chapter (1999)
  • National Safety Council of India's Industry Safety Awards (2003, 2004)
