About Niagara

With decades of experience and extensive customer research, Niagara is committed to helping customers create their ideal bath fittings, recognizing that each space reflects personal style. We specialize in complete bath fittings, offering an extensive range that includes faucets, showerheads and other innovative fittings, all crafted with high-quality components and advanced technology. Our presence is felt throughout South India, backed by a robust distributor network that has helped us build a nationwide customer base of millions of loyal supporters.
