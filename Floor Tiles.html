About Our Floor Tiles

Whether renovating your home or creating a new space, our tiles are designed to ensure longevity, visual charm and easy maintenance.

Our floor tiles blend strength and refinement, making them suitable for high-usage areas and luxurious spaces in residential and commercial settings. Designed to withstand everyday wear, they maintain their visual appeal and functionality over time.

Some of our tiles are demonstrated using experiments to prove their high performance. SHG Tiles resist scratches in tests with hard objects. Cool Roof Tiles reflect over 85% of solar rays, helping reduce heat. Anti-slippery tiles are tested with water and oil to ensure safe footing in wet conditions.

Why Choose Anuj Tiles for Your Floor?

With various designs and finishes available, you can easily find the perfect match for your space. Anuj Tiles are perfectly suited for a range of applications, including flooring, walls and decorative areas, engineered for exceptional durability. With low water absorption, enhanced surface hardness and superior breaking strength (Modulus of Rupture), our tiles showcase lasting endurance. Elevate your interiors with our selection, ensuring that your investment not only enhances visual appeal but also stands the test of time.

Quality Guarantee: At Anuj Tiles, we utilize advanced Vibropresses and Hermetic Presses, along with both Wet Cast and Dry Cast techniques, to ensure superior product quality. Our in-house testing laboratory rigorously evaluates compressive strength, transverse strength, water absorption and abrasion resistance ensuring that every tile meets the highest industry standards.
