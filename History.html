1996 - Founding

Founded as a partnership firm specializing in the trading of tiles and sanitary ware.

1996-2000 - Early Growth

Achieved steady growth, establishing a strong reputation as a reliable supplier in the market.

2001 - Market Leadership

Attained a leadership position in dealer sales across Tamil Nadu.

2006 - Corporate Expansion

  • Expanded operations by establishing Aravind Ceramic Industries Limited (ACIL), marking the entry into manufacturing, with Uthiramerur, Kanchipuram serving as ACIL Unit 1.
  • Launched the Anuj Tiles brand, further diversifying the product portfolio.

2008 - Manufacturing Initiation

Commenced manufacturing operations, becoming the first tile manufacturer in Tamil Nadu with an initial capacity of 6,000 sqm/day.

2012 - Corporate Restructuring

Underwent corporate restructuring, forming Aravind Ceramics Private Ltd (ACPL).

2013 - Product Diversification

Introduced parking tiles as part of product diversification and expanded production capacity to 4,500 sqm/day.

2015 - Technological Advancement

  • Integrated digital printing technology, elevating product design and quality. Expanded capacity further with a third production line to 10,000 sqm/day.
  • Launched PGVT/GVT (Polished Glazed Vitrified Tiles).

2016 - Digital Innovation

Continued to lead in innovation by introducing digital parking tiles.

2017 - Market Dominance

  • Solidified market dominance, becoming the sole tile manufacturer in Tamil Nadu.
  • Broadened product range with the addition of Cool Roof tiles.

2020 - Product Expansion

Expanded the product range, launching manhole covers, sink and kitchen slab tiles, SHG and HG tiles.

2021 - Acquisition

Acquired Regma Ceramics Pvt. Limited, increasing manufacturing capacity by an additional 12,000 sqm/day; Regma Ceramics serves as ACIL Unit 3.

2022 - Corporate Growth

Established the corporate office in Porur, a procurement office in Morbi, showrooms throughout Tamil Nadu and distributors across South India.

2023 - New Facility

  • Established a new manufacturing facility ACIL Unit 2 in Thanjavur, with a production capacity of 20,000 sqm/day.
  • Total production capacity now stands at 52,500 sqm/day, reflecting ongoing growth and commitment to innovation.

2024 - Present

We are well-represented across South India to serve you better, striving to meet your needs and continuing to grow.
