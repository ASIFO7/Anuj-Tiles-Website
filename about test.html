A LEGACY OF EXCELLENCE

With over 30+ years of expertise, Aravind Ceramics Private Ltd., the company behind the ANUJ Tiles brand, is synonymous with quality, innovation and customer trust. Headquartered in Chennai, we are proud to be the only premier vitrified tiles manufacturer in Tamil Nadu, delivering high-quality products that transform residential and commercial spaces.

Our extensive range of over 6,000+ tile designs seamlessly blends modern and traditional styles, ensuring elegance and functionality in every room. Whether you are creating a home or a commercial project, our tiles add a distinctive touch of beauty and durability.

In addition to our impressive tile collections, we offer a full suite of home improvement products, including sanitary ware, bath fittings and kitchen accessories, making us a one-stop solution for all design needs.

Our dedicated team of experts is here to provide you with the best possible customer experience, from helping you choose the perfect product to delivering it right to your doorstep. With our commitment to excellence, you can rest assured you are getting the best value for your money.

Our production capacity of 52,500 sqm/day reflects our commitment to quality and innovation. With advanced R&D, we continually introduce new products to the market.

As South India’s No.1 Brand, we stand out for our affordable, high-quality products and commitment to sustainability.
